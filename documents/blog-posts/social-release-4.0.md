# Flow Tool Kit 4.0: Social Posts

## LinkedIn post

We just shipped the biggest release in Flow Tool Kit's history. Here is the magic in 60 seconds.

Flow Tool Kit 4.0 is live: forty-six releases of capability in one upgrade, and it changes what a "form" can be on Salesforce.

🧭 Stages Mode: long applications become a stage overview. A family finishes a housing application across three evenings and two phones, while your staff watch progress as real Salesforce records they can report on and automate.

💾 Autosave: work saves itself as people type, with a quiet "Draft saved" note keeping them confident. Nobody retypes an hour of answers, and guests are covered too.

📊 Likert sections: a battery of same-scale questions becomes one clean grid, scored live, with the total stamped into a real field your automation can react to instantly.

💚 Number overrides: preset amount chips, steppers, and sliders with true currency formatting. A donation amount is a decision, not a data entry task.

🏷️ Picklist option labels: respondents see friendly, even personalized wording (merge fields re-render live) while your stored values stay API-safe.

✅ Boolean overrides: a checkbox becomes an attestation card, a rich-text consent statement that fills in your brand color when accepted.

🎨 Divider styles: twelve designed, theme-driven dividers with labels and icons, so long forms read like documents instead of walls of fields.

💬 Rich text message cards: guidance renders as info, warning, error, and success cards that match your theme.
